A Closer Look at Missouri’s Native Species

This Bird Guide introduces a variety of native Missouri bird species found across the state’s diverse landscapes. From colorful songbirds in forest canopies to waterfowl in wetlands and raptors soaring above open prairies, Missouri provides habitat for an impressive range of birds throughout the year.

This section will explore key identification features, behaviors, and habitats of common native species. As the site expands, detailed profiles will provide additional information about appearance, diet, nesting habits, and seasonal movement patterns.
